BMW X5 Description

Extended Information

Technical Specifications

    Engine: 3.0L TwinPower Turbo Inline-6 with mild hybrid technology

    Power: 375 hp

    Torque: 398 lb-ft

    Transmission: All models are equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission

    Acceleration (0-60 mph): 5.3 seconds

    Fuel Economy: 21 mpg city / 26 mpg highway

    Cargo Space: 33.9 cubic feet behind the second row

Design

    Exterior: Front Fascia: The iconic kidney grille, optionally illuminated, and slim adaptive LED headlights. Lighting: Optional BMW Laserlight technology for enhanced nighttime visibility. Off-Road Package: Adds skid plates and increased ride height for rugged terrain.

    Interior: Material Quality: Premium leather, real wood, and aluminum trim options. Display: Curved display system featuring a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and a 14.9-inch infotainment screen. Ambient Lighting: Customizable LED lighting enhances the cabin atmosphere. Third Row (Optional): Suitable for children or short trips, adds flexibility for larger families.

Systems and Technology

    Premium audio systems, including the optional Bowers & Wilkins Diamond Surround Sound System.

    Forward Collision Warning with Automatic Emergency Braking.

    Blind-Spot Monitoring.

    Lane Departure Warning.

    Highway Assistant for hands-free driving on certain roads.

    Parking Assistant Plus with a 360-degree camera.

    Adaptive Cruise Control with Stop & Go.

Cons

    Third-Row Limitations: The optional third-row seating is cramped and suitable only for small passengers or short trips.

    Fuel Efficiency: V8-powered models, like the M60i and X5 M, have below-average fuel economy compared to rivals.

    Ride Comfort (Sportier Trims): Performance-oriented models may feel firmer and less comfortable on rough roads.

    Complex Infotainment System: The iDrive system, while powerful, can have a learning curve for new users.
