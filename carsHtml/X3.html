BMW X3 Description

Extended Information

Technical Specifications

    Engine Type: 2.0L TwinPower Turbocharged Inline-4

    Power Output: 248 hp

    Torque: 258 lb-ft

    Transmission: 8-speed automatic transmission

    Acceleration (0-60 mph): 6.3 seconds

    Fuel Economy: 23 mpg city / 29 mpg highway

    Cargo Space: 28.7 cubic feet behind the rear seats, 62.7 cubic feet with the rear seats folded

Design

    Exterior: Front Fascia: A signature kidney grille with optional illuminated grille on some trims. Lighting: Adaptive LED headlights are standard, with optional Laserlight technology for enhanced visibility. Aerodynamics: Sculpted body lines enhance aerodynamics and aesthetic appeal.

    Interior: Material Quality: Premium materials like Vernasca leather and fine wood or aluminum trims. Seats: Comfortable and supportive seats with available ventilated and heated options. Display: A 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and a 12.3-inch touchscreen display (on higher trims). Ambient Lighting: Customizable colors to match your mood.

Systems and Technology

    Infotainment System: Voice assistant with natural language processing ("Hey BMW" functionality). Optional Gesture Control for hands-free operation.

    Driver Assistance and Safety Systems: Active Driving Assistant (lane departure warning, forward collision warning, pedestrian detection). Parking sensors (front and rear).

    Performance Features: Available xDrive (all-wheel-drive system) for improved traction and stability in various conditions. Dynamic Stability Control and Variable Sport Steering.

Cons

    Firm Ride on Sporty Models: The X3 M and M40i prioritize performance, leading to a stiffer ride compared to some competitors.

    No Third Row: Unlike some competitors, the X3 is strictly a five-seater, which may limit its appeal to larger families.

    Limited Electric Range (PHEV): The plug-in hybrid X3 xDrive30e has a relatively short electric-only range of around 20 miles, which might not satisfy those seeking more EV capability.
